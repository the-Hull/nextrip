Where are you headed for your NexTrip? ✈


NexTrip helps you find your next destination for a well deserved break after 12 weeks of intense coding, problem-solving and head-scratching.
NexTrip uses Non-negatve Matrix Factoring (NMF) to predict destination ratings based on your preferences from similar users (calculated with Euclidian Distances).
Find out more about the statistics in the background here.

1. Introduce Yourself 👋🏽

Set your Big 5 Personality Traits. We'll use these to identify similar travellers based on your character and your age group.

2. Set your Style 🥾🏖️

Choose your typical travel style(s), like Urban Explorer, Foodie or Thrill Seeker. We'll use these to match you with similar travellers, combined with your personality and age.

3. Rate Destinations 💯✔️

Tell us which destinations you love or dislike. We'll use these to inform our predictions based on destination ratings from the 500 most similar users in our database.
